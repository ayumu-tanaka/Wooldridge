# SAVING データセットの説明

**観測数（Obs）:** 100

## 変数一覧

| 変数名 | 説明 |
|------|------|
| sav | annual savings, $ (1970) |
| inc | annual income, $  (1970) |
| size | family size |
| educ | years education, household head |
| age | age of household head |
| black | household head is black の場合1 |
| cons | annual consumption, $ (1970) |
